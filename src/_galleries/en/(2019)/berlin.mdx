On August 23, 2019, I flew from Amsterdam to Berlin, Germany.

On the day I arrived, I visited the Spy Museum, which was located very close to my hotel.

The next day, I hadn’t done much research because I was short on sleep, but I decided to at least go see the Berlin Wall.

After that, I wandered around Potsdamer Platz, and was drawn to a building called the Sony Center.

The Eisbein I had there was incredibly delicious.

Later on, I found out that the Blue Man Group—who were quite popular for a while—happened to have a show that night,

so I impulsively bought a ticket and went to see it.
