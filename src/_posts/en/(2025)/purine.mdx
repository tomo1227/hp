This time, I seriously photographed food at a popular pudding restaurant I visited in Hokkaido. The only equipment I used was the RICOH GR IIIx. No strobes or LEDs were used, and I only relied on the ambient light in the restaurant for the shots.

First, here’s a shot of the bagna càuda. I'll start by showing some casual shots.
